Chaotic attractor
Attractor representing the dynamics of an acoustic-driven cavity flow.
For high acoustic forcing, the flow adopt more complex behaviors, represented in the phase space by a chaotic attractor. This chaotic dynamics implies that the flow is, due to sensitivity to initial conditions, unpredictable on the long term.
