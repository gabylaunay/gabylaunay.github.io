Velocity field in a HSV
Velocity field upstream from an emerging obstacle obtained by PIV.
Measurement is taken in the vertical upstream plane of symmetry and show two swirling structures: (i) the HSV, created in the bottom region by the boundary layer separation, and (ii) the superior TFC, created by the first one at the corner between the obstacle and the free-surface.
