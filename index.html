PIV/PLIF

Vorticity fields in a HSV
Vorticity field measured by PIV in the vertical plane of symmetry of a horseshoe vortex developing around an emerging obstacle.
The vortices composing the horseshoe vortex are clearly visible as minima of vorticity.
This representaation allows to track the vortices in time and to have qualitative information on the flow dynamics.
FPIV in supercritical flows
PIVF (Particle Image Velocimetry by Fluorescence) in the vertical plan of a super-critical flow Fr>1 around an emerging obstacle.
PIVF allows to remove the scattered part of the laser emission, and make measurement in the presence of a strongly deformed and agitated free-surface.

Flow Dynamics

Oscillation direction and amplitude
Oscillation direction and amplitude due to the horseshoe vortex in a flow around an emerging obstacle.
Extracting the oscillation due to a particular structure (here the HSV) at a given frequency allows to visualize the impact of this structure on the surrounding flow.
