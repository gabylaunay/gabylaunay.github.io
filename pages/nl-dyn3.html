Poincare section
Poincare section of an attractor representing the dynamics of an acoustic-driven cavity flow.
Right: representation of the attractor in the three-dimensional phase space. Left: Density map of the Poincare section in one of the main attractor direction. The Poincare section helps reducing the dimension number and allow to get a qualitative insight on attractor properties (fractality, dimensions, chaotic behavior).
