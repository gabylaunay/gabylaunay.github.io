Oscillation direction and amplitude
Oscillation directions and amplitudes induced by the horseshoe vortex in a flow around an emerging obstacle.
Measure is taken in the horizontal plane, near the bottom, to observe the evolution of the horseshoe vortex as it roll around the obstacle. Extracting the oscillation due to a structure (here the HSV) at a particular frequency allows to visualize the impact of this structure on the surrounding flow.
