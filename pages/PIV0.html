Velocity field in a HSV
2D Velocity field obtained by PIV upstream from an emerging obstacle.
Measure is taken in the vertical plane of symmetry, upstream from the obstacle, and show two swirling structures: (i) the HSV, created in the bottom region by the boundary layer separation, and (ii) the superior TFC, at the corner between the obstacle and the free-surface, induced by the first one.
