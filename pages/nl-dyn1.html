Non-chaotic attractor
Attractor representing the dynamics of an acoustic-driven cavity flow.
This attractor is obtained using the delay embedding method on the velocity evolution at a specific point of the flow. This method allows to extract the underlying dynamics, despite the apparent complexity of the velocity time-series. This dynamics is represented here by a non-chaotic torus.
